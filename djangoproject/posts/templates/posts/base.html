The Western Australian

A joint volunteer project between the Western Australian Genealogical Society (WAGS) and the Army Museum of Western Australia (AMWA).

What is W.A.M.D.L?

A digital public library that reveals the many unique and previously unseen historical records and personal papers relating to Western Australian military heritage in order to make them freely and widely accessible to a state, national and international audience..
